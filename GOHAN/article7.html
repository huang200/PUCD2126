# FASHION

“We’re gonna need a bigger boat.”

French fashion house Givenchy — now under the creative direction of Clare Waight Keller — have re-released items with its famous shark motif. Taking on the Great White shark as its latest mascot, the shark can be found emblazoned on a black ‘Cuban’ cut T-shirt, cotton cap and canvas and leather backpack.

All three items can be purchased over at MR PORTER now ranging from $1,345 USD for the backpack; $550 USD for the T-shirt; and $495 USD for the cap.
